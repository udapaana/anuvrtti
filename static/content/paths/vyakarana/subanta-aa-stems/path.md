## concept - @term[strīliṅga] @deva[आ]-stems

Stems ending in @deva[आ] form the largest class of @term[strīliṅga] (feminine) nouns in Sanskrit. Most feminine adjectives and many common nouns follow this pattern.

**@deva[सेना] (army, f.) — full paradigm:**

| Case  | Singular        | Dual              | Plural          |
| ----- | --------------- | ----------------- | --------------- |
| Nom.  | @deva[सेना]     | @deva[सेने]       | @deva[सेनाः]    |
| Acc.  | @deva[सेनाम्]   | @deva[सेने]       | @deva[सेनाः]    |
| Inst. | @deva[सेनया]    | @deva[सेनाभ्याम्] | @deva[सेनाभिः]  |
| Dat.  | @deva[सेनायै]   | @deva[सेनाभ्याम्] | @deva[सेनाभ्यः] |
| Abl.  | @deva[सेनायाः]  | @deva[सेनाभ्याम्] | @deva[सेनाभ्यः] |
| Gen.  | @deva[सेनायाः]  | @deva[सेनयोः]     | @deva[सेनानाम्] |
| Loc.  | @deva[सेनायाम्] | @deva[सेनयोः]     | @deva[सेनासु]   |
| Voc.  | @deva[सेने]     | @deva[सेने]       | @deva[सेनाः]    |

**Comparison with masculine @deva[अ]-stems (@deva[देव]):**

| Case      | @deva[देव] (m.) | @deva[सेना] (f.) | Key difference               |
| --------- | --------------- | ---------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Nom. Sg.  | @deva[देवः]     | @deva[सेना]      | no @deva[विसर्ग]             |
| Inst. Sg. | @deva[देवेन]    | @deva[सेनया]     | @deva[-एन] vs. @deva[-या]    |
| Dat. Sg.  | @deva[देवाय]    | @deva[सेनायै]    | @deva[-आय] vs. @deva[-आयै]   |
| Abl. Sg.  | @deva[देवात्]   | @deva[सेनायाः]   | @deva[-आत्] vs. @deva[-आयाः] |
| Gen. Sg.  | @deva[देवस्य]   | @deva[सेनायाः]   | @deva[-स्य] vs. @deva[-आयाः] |
| Loc. Sg.  | @deva[देवे]     | @deva[सेनायाम्]  | @deva[-ए] vs. @deva[-आयाम्]  |

## @ref[7.3.105] - आङि चापः

**Key Terms:** @deva[ओस्], @deva[आ], @deva[ए]

Before the case-ending @deva[ओस्] (Gen./Loc. dual) and before @deva[आ] of the Instrumental singular, @deva[ए] substitutes for the final @deva[आ] of the feminine stem.

This produces the characteristic @deva[-या] and @deva[-योः] forms:

- @deva[सेना] + @deva[आ] (Inst.) → @deva[सेन] + @deva[ए] + @deva[आ] → @deva[सेनया] (by @deva[यण्] sandhi)
- @deva[सेना] + @deva[ओस्] (Gen./Loc. Du.) → @deva[सेन] + @deva[ए] + @deva[ओस्] → @deva[सेनयोः]
- @deva[विद्या] + @deva[आ] → @deva[विद्यया]
- @deva[कन्या] + @deva[ओस्] → @deva[कन्ययोः]

---

## @ref[7.3.106] - सम्बुद्धौ च

**Key Terms:** @deva[सम्बुद्धि], @deva[आ]

In Vocative singular, @deva[ए] substitutes for the final @deva[आ]:

- @deva[हे सेने] "O army!"
- @deva[हे विद्ये] "O knowledge!"
- @deva[हे कन्ये] "O maiden!"
- @deva[हे लक्ष्मि] — wait, this is @deva[ई]-stem (different pattern)

This @deva[ए]-ending vocative is distinctive of @deva[आ]-stems.

---

## @ref[6.1.102] - प्रथमयोः पूर्वसवर्णः

**Key Terms:** @deva[प्रथमा], @deva[आ]

In Nominative and Accusative (the "first" cases), the stem-final vowel and the ending merge by @deva[सवर्णदीर्घ] (like-vowel lengthening):

- @deva[सेना] + @deva[सु] (Nom. Sg.) → @deva[सेना] (the @deva[आ] absorbs @deva[स्] which drops)
- @deva[सेना] + @deva[अम्] (Acc. Sg.) → @deva[सेनाम्]

---

## @ref[7.1.18] - औङ आपः

**Key Terms:** @deva[औ], @deva[आप्]

Before @deva[औ] (Nom./Acc. dual ending), @deva[आप्]-marked feminine stems (which includes @deva[आ]-stems created by @deva[टाप्], @deva[डाप्], @deva[चाप्]) take a specific form:

- @deva[सेना] + @deva[औ] → @deva[सेने] "the two armies"
- @deva[विद्या] + @deva[औ] → @deva[विद्ये] "the two knowledges"

The @deva[आ] + @deva[औ] merges to @deva[ए].

---

## @ref[7.3.104] - ओसि च

**Key Terms:** @deva[ओस्], @deva[आ]

Before @deva[ओस्] endings (Gen./Loc. dual), the @deva[आ]-stem sandhi applies, producing the @deva[-योः] ending as described in @ref[7.3.105].

---

## @ref[7.3.112] - आण्नद्याः

**Key Terms:** @deva[आम्], @deva[नदी]

The Genitive plural ending @deva[आम्] applies after @deva[आ]-stems and @deva[नदी]-stems:

- @deva[सेनानाम्] "of the armies"
- @deva[विद्यानाम्] "of the knowledges"
- @deva[कन्यानाम्] "of the maidens"

The @deva[आ] of the stem combines with the @deva[आम्] ending.

---

## @ref[7.1.54] - ह्रस्वनद्यापो नुट्

**Key Terms:** @deva[ह्रस्व], @deva[नदी], @deva[नुट्]

Before the Genitive plural @deva[आम्], short feminine stems, @deva[नदी]-stems, and @deva[आप्]-stems take the @deva[नुट्] augment (= @deva[न्]):

- @deva[सेना] + @deva[नुट्] + @deva[आम्] → @deva[सेनानाम्]

This @deva[न्] insertion distinguishes feminine Gen. Pl. from masculine (@deva[देवानाम्] has @deva[न्] from a different source).

---

## @ref[7.1.50] - आज्जसेरसुक्

**Key Terms:** @deva[आ], @deva[जस्], @deva[असुक्]

After @deva[आ]-final stems, the Nominative plural ending @deva[जस्] (= @deva[अस्]) takes the augment @deva[असुक्], effectively producing @deva[आः]:

- @deva[सेनाः] "the armies"
- @deva[विद्याः] "the knowledges"
- @deva[कन्याः] "the maidens"
