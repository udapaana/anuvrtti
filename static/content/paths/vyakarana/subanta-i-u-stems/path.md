## concept - @deva[इकारान्त] and @deva[उकारान्त] Stems

Stems ending in @deva[इ] / @deva[ई] and @deva[उ] / @deva[ऊ] form one of the most important declension classes. The key feature is vowel gradation: the stem-final @term[svara] undergoes @term[guṇa] (→ @deva[ए] / @deva[ओ]) or @term[vṛddhi] (→ @deva[ऐ] / @deva[औ]) in certain cases, and @term[yaṇ] substitution (@deva[इ] → @deva[य्], @deva[उ] → @deva[व्]) before @term[ac]-initial endings.

**@deva[अग्नि] (fire, m.) — full paradigm:**

| Case  | Singular       | Dual               | Plural           |
| ----- | -------------- | ------------------ | ---------------- |
| Nom.  | @deva[अग्निः]  | @deva[अग्नी]       | @deva[अग्नयः]    |
| Acc.  | @deva[अग्निम्] | @deva[अग्नी]       | @deva[अग्नीन्]   |
| Inst. | @deva[अग्निना] | @deva[अग्निभ्याम्] | @deva[अग्निभिः]  |
| Dat.  | @deva[अग्नये]  | @deva[अग्निभ्याम्] | @deva[अग्निभ्यः] |
| Abl.  | @deva[अग्नेः]  | @deva[अग्निभ्याम्] | @deva[अग्निभ्यः] |
| Gen.  | @deva[अग्नेः]  | @deva[अग्न्योः]    | @deva[अग्नीनाम्] |
| Loc.  | @deva[अग्नौ]   | @deva[अग्न्योः]    | @deva[अग्निषु]   |
| Voc.  | @deva[अग्ने]   | @deva[अग्नी]       | @deva[अग्नयः]    |

**@deva[गुरु] (teacher, m.) — full paradigm:**

| Case  | Singular      | Dual              | Plural          |
| ----- | ------------- | ----------------- | --------------- |
| Nom.  | @deva[गुरुः]  | @deva[गुरू]       | @deva[गुरवः]    |
| Acc.  | @deva[गुरुम्] | @deva[गुरू]       | @deva[गुरून्]   |
| Inst. | @deva[गुरुणा] | @deva[गुरुभ्याम्] | @deva[गुरुभिः]  |
| Dat.  | @deva[गुरवे]  | @deva[गुरुभ्याम्] | @deva[गुरुभ्यः] |
| Abl.  | @deva[गुरोः]  | @deva[गुरुभ्याम्] | @deva[गुरुभ्यः] |
| Gen.  | @deva[गुरोः]  | @deva[गुर्वोः]    | @deva[गुरूणाम्] |
| Loc.  | @deva[गुरौ]   | @deva[गुर्वोः]    | @deva[गुरुषु]   |
| Voc.  | @deva[गुरो]   | @deva[गुरू]       | @deva[गुरवः]    |

Notice the parallel patterns: @deva[अग्नये] / @deva[गुरवे] (Dat.), @deva[अग्नेः] / @deva[गुरोः] (Abl./Gen.), @deva[अग्नौ] / @deva[गुरौ] (Loc.).

## @ref[7.3.111] - घेर्ङिति

**Key Terms:** @deva[घि], @deva[गुण]

For the @deva[इ] and @deva[उ] of stems called @deva[घि], @deva[गुण] substitutes before @deva[ङित्] (Dative, Ablative, Genitive singular) and related endings.

@deva[घि] stems are short @deva[इ]- and @deva[उ]-stems (not @deva[ई] or @deva[ऊ]):

| Stem         | Case      | @deva[गुण]          | Form          |
| ------------ | --------- | ------------------- | ------------- |
| @deva[अग्नि] | Dat.      | @deva[इ] → @deva[ए] | @deva[अग्नये] |
| @deva[अग्नि] | Abl./Gen. | @deva[इ] → @deva[ए] | @deva[अग्नेः] |
| @deva[गुरु]  | Dat.      | @deva[उ] → @deva[ओ] | @deva[गुरवे]  |
| @deva[गुरु]  | Abl./Gen. | @deva[उ] → @deva[ओ] | @deva[गुरोः]  |
| @deva[वायु]  | Dat.      | @deva[उ] → @deva[ओ] | @deva[वायवे]  |

The @deva[ए] / @deva[ओ] then undergoes @deva[अयादेश] before vowel-initial endings (e.g., @deva[ए] → @deva[अय्] before @deva[ए]).

## @ref[7.3.110] - ऋतो ङिसर्वनामस्थानयोः

**Key Terms:** @deva[ऋ], @deva[गुण], @deva[सर्वनामस्थान]

@deva[गुण] substitutes for final @deva[ऋ] of a stem in Locative singular and in strong cases. Though primarily for @deva[ऋ]-stems, this rule shows the pattern of vowel gradation that parallels @deva[इ] / @deva[उ] stems:

- @deva[पितृ] → @deva[पितरि] (Loc.), @deva[पितरौ] (Nom. Du.)
- @deva[मातृ] → @deva[मातरि] (Loc.), @deva[मातरः] (Nom. Pl.)

## @ref[7.1.73] - इकोऽचि विभक्तौ

**Key Terms:** @deva[इक्], @deva[अच्], @deva[विभक्ति]

The augment @deva[नुम्] is added to a **neuter** stem ending in @deva[इक्] (@deva[इ], @deva[उ], @deva[ऋ], @deva[ऌ]) before a vowel-initial case ending.

This is specific to **neuter** @deva[इ]- and @deva[उ]-stems:

- @deva[वारि] (water, n.) + @deva[इ] (Loc.) → @deva[वारिणि] "in water"
- @deva[वारि] + @deva[आ] (Inst.) → @deva[वारिणा] "by water"
- @deva[मधु] (honey, n.) + @deva[इ] (Loc.) → @deva[मधुनि] "in honey"
- @deva[मधु] + @deva[आ] (Inst.) → @deva[मधुना] "by honey"

Masculine @deva[इ]- and @deva[उ]-stems do **not** take @deva[नुम्] in these cases: @deva[अग्निना] (not \*@deva[अग्निणा]).

## @ref[6.1.77] - इको यणचि

**Key Terms:** @deva[इक्], @deva[यण्], @deva[अच्]

The semi-vowels @deva[य्], @deva[व्], @deva[र्], @deva[ल्] substitute for @deva[इ], @deva[उ], @deva[ऋ], @deva[ऌ] when followed by a dissimilar vowel. This is the fundamental sandhi rule for @deva[इ]/@deva[उ]-stems:

| Stem vowel           | Before vowel                               | Example         |
| -------------------- | ------------------------------------------ | --------------- |
| @deva[इ] → @deva[य्] | @deva[अग्नि] + @deva[ओः] → @deva[अग्न्योः] | Gen./Loc. Du.   |
| @deva[उ] → @deva[व्] | @deva[गुरु] + @deva[ओः] → @deva[गुर्वोः]   | Gen./Loc. Du.   |
| @deva[इ] → @deva[य्] | @deva[दधि] + @deva[अत्र] → @deva[दध्यत्र]  | compound sandhi |
| @deva[उ] → @deva[व्] | @deva[मधु] + @deva[अत्र] → @deva[मध्वत्र]  | compound sandhi |

## @ref[7.1.75] - अस्थिदधिसक्थ्यक्ष्णामनङुदात्तः

**Key Terms:** @deva[अस्थि], @deva[दधि], @deva[अनङ्]

The substitute @deva[अन्] (@deva[अनङ्]) replaces the finals of four special neuter stems — @deva[अस्थि] (bone), @deva[दधि] (curd), @deva[सक्थि] (thigh), @deva[अक्षि] (eye) — before endings from Instrumental onward that begin with a vowel.

| Stem         | Nom.         | Inst.          | Loc.                           |
| ------------ | ------------ | -------------- | ------------------------------ |
| @deva[अस्थि] | @deva[अस्थि] | @deva[अस्थ्ना] | @deva[अस्थ्नि] / @deva[अस्थनि] |
| @deva[दधि]   | @deva[दधि]   | @deva[दध्ना]   | @deva[दध्नि] / @deva[दधनि]     |
| @deva[अक्षि] | @deva[अक्षि] | @deva[अक्ष्णा] | @deva[अक्ष्णि] / @deva[अक्षणि] |

These are irregular and must be learned as exceptions.

## @ref[6.4.77] - अचि श्नुधातुभ्रुवां य्वोरियङुवङौ

**Key Terms:** @deva[अच्], @deva[इयङ्], @deva[उवङ्]

Before vowel-initial endings, @deva[इ] becomes @deva[इय्] and @deva[उ] becomes @deva[उव्] in certain stems (specifically @deva[श्नु]-class roots, monosyllabic stems, and @deva[भ्रू]):

- @deva[भ्रू] (eyebrow) → @deva[भ्रुवः] (Gen. Sg.)
- @deva[स्त्री] (woman) → special declension with @deva[इय्] in some forms

---

## @ref[7.1.70] - उगिदचां सर्वनामस्थानेऽधातोः

**Key Terms:** @deva[उगित्], @deva[सर्वनामस्थान], @deva[नुम्]

Stems with indicatory @deva[उ], @deva[ऋ], @deva[ऌ] and @deva[अञ्च्]-type stems get @deva[नुम्] augment in strong cases. This applies to participial stems like @deva[भवत्]:

- @deva[भवान्], @deva[भवन्तौ], @deva[भवन्तः]
- @deva[श्रेयान्], @deva[श्रेयांसौ], @deva[श्रेयांसः]

| @deva[ऋषि]   | m.     | seer          | person                |

| @deva[गुरु]  | m.     | teacher       | person                |
| @deva[शत्रु] | m.     | enemy         | person                |
| @deva[पशु]   | m.     | animal        | being                 |

| @deva[वारि]  | n.     | water         | element               |
| @deva[मधु]   | n.     | honey         | substance             |
| @deva[दधि]   | n.     | curd          | substance (irregular) |
