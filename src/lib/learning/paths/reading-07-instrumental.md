## concept - The Instrumental Case

The instrumental (@deva[तृतीया]) expresses:
- **Means/instrument**: "by" or "with"
- **Agent** in passive: "by whom"
- **Accompaniment**: "together with"

@deva[हस्तेन लिखति] — "writes **with** (his) hand"

---

## concept - Instrumental Endings

| Gender | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|--------|----------|------|--------|
| Masc. (देव) | @deva[देवेन] | @deva[देवाभ्याम्] | @deva[देवैः] |
| Neut. (फल) | @deva[फलेन] | @deva[फलाभ्याम्] | @deva[फलैः] |
| Fem. (कन्या) | @deva[कन्यया] | @deva[कन्याभ्याम्] | @deva[कन्याभिः] |

**Key markers:** @deva[-एन] (sg.), @deva[-आभ्याम्] (du.), @deva[-ऐः/-भिः] (pl.)

---

## concept - Meanings of Instrumental

**1. Instrument/means:**
- @deva[शस्त्रेण हन्ति] — "kills with a weapon"
- @deva[वाचा वदति] — "speaks with words"

**2. Cause:**
- @deva[दुःखेन रोदिति] — "cries from sorrow"
- @deva[भयेन पलायते] — "flees from fear"

**3. Accompaniment (with सह or alone):**
- @deva[पुत्रेण सह गच्छति] — "goes with (his) son"
- @deva[मित्रैः आगच्छति] — "comes with friends"

---

## concept - Recognition

When you see these endings, think "by/with":

| Ending | Case |
|--------|------|
| @deva[-एन] | inst. sg. masc/neut |
| @deva[-या] | inst. sg. fem |
| @deva[-आभ्याम्] | inst. dual (all) |
| @deva[-ऐः] | inst. pl. masc/neut |
| @deva[-आभिः] | inst. pl. fem |

---

## concept - Practice Sentences

1. @deva[नरः अश्वेन गच्छति]
   - "The man goes **by horse**"

2. @deva[बालकः पुस्तकेन पठति]
   - "The boy reads **with a book**"

3. @deva[राजा सैन्येन युध्यति]
   - "The king fights **with an army**"

4. @deva[कविः कलया रमते]
   - "The poet delights **in art**"

## concept - Lesson Summary

The instrumental case (@deva[तृतीया]) shows:
- The tool/means: @deva[हस्तेन] "with hand"
- The cause: @deva[भयेन] "from fear"
- Accompaniment: @deva[मित्रैः] "with friends"

Endings: @deva[-एन] (sg.), @deva[-ऐः] (pl.)
