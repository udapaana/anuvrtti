## concept - Person and Number

Verbs change based on WHO is acting and HOW MANY:

| Person | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|--------|----------|------|--------|
| 3rd (he/she/it) | @deva[-ति] | @deva[-तः] | @deva[-न्ति] |
| 2nd (you) | @deva[-सि] | @deva[-थः] | @deva[-थ] |
| 1st (I/we) | @deva[-मि] | @deva[-वः] | @deva[-मः] |

---

## concept - Third Person (Most Common)

You'll see third person most often in texts:

| | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|--|----------|------|--------|
| @deva[गच्छ्] | @deva[गच्छति] | @deva[गच्छतः] | @deva[गच्छन्ति] |
| | "he goes" | "they two go" | "they go" |

---

## concept - First and Second Person

For dialogue and direct speech:

**First person (I/we):**
- @deva[गच्छामि] — "I go"
- @deva[गच्छावः] — "we two go"
- @deva[गच्छामः] — "we go"

**Second person (you):**
- @deva[गच्छसि] — "you go"
- @deva[गच्छथः] — "you two go"
- @deva[गच्छथ] — "you (all) go"

## concept - Common Verbs

Master these high-frequency verbs:

| Root | Meaning | He/She | They |
|------|---------|--------|------|
| @deva[गम्] | go | @deva[गच्छति] | @deva[गच्छन्ति] |
| @deva[वद्] | speak | @deva[वदति] | @deva[वदन्ति] |
| @deva[पठ्] | read | @deva[पठति] | @deva[पठन्ति] |
| @deva[लिख्] | write | @deva[लिखति] | @deva[लिखन्ति] |
| @deva[दृश्] | see | @deva[पश्यति] | @deva[पश्यन्ति] |
| @deva[श्रु] | hear | @deva[शृणोति] | @deva[शृण्वन्ति] |

## concept - Practice Sentences

1. @deva[बालकाः पठन्ति] — "The boys read"
2. @deva[अहं गच्छामि] — "I go"
3. @deva[त्वं पश्यसि] — "You see"
4. @deva[वयं वदामः] — "We speak"
