## 3.3.10 - Tumun ṇvulau kriyāyāṃ kriyārthāyām

**Key Terms:** tumun, kriyA, kriyArTA

The suffix 'tumun' (= tum) expresses purpose - an action done for the sake of another action.

---

## 3.4.65 - Śeṣātkartari parasmaipadam

**Key Terms:** tumun, kartari

The infinitive is used when the agent of the main and subordinate verbs is the same.

---

## 1.1.40 - Ktvātosunkasunaḥ

**Key Terms:** tumun, avyaya

Like ktva, tumun forms are avyaya (indeclinable). They don't inflect.

---

## 7.2.35 - Ārdhadhātukasyeḍ valādeḥ

**Key Terms:** ArDaDAtukaH, iT

The augment 'iṭ' is added before tumun when it follows consonant-final roots. "kartum, gantum."

---

## 6.4.66 - Ghumastvanas...

**Key Terms:** Gum, tumun

Roots ending in 'ghu' (dā, dhā, etc.) have special treatment before tumun.

---

## 6.1.15 - Vacivapivayati...

**Key Terms:** vac, vap, tumun

Roots vac, vap, etc. show samprasāraṇa (semivowel → vowel) before tumun.

---

## 7.2.44 - Svaratisūtidhṛṅ...

**Key Terms:** svara, sU, tumun

Certain roots have optional 'iṭ' before tumun. Creates doublets.

---

## 7.3.84 - Sārvadhātukārdhadhātukayoḥ

**Key Terms:** guRa, tumun

Guna applies to the root vowel before tumun (ārdhadhātuka). Core strengthening.
