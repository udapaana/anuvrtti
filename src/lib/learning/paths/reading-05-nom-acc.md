## concept - The Two Essential Cases

Most sentences need just two cases:
- **Nominative** (@deva[प्रथमा]) — the subject, who acts
- **Accusative** (@deva[द्वितीया]) — the object, what's acted upon

@deva[नरः फलं खादति]
- @deva[नरः] = nominative (the man — subject)
- @deva[फलम्] = accusative (the fruit — object)

---

## concept - Masculine a-stems (देव type)

| Case | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|------|----------|------|--------|
| Nom. | @deva[देवः] | @deva[देवौ] | @deva[देवाः] |
| Acc. | @deva[देवम्] | @deva[देवौ] | @deva[देवान्] |

**Pattern:** Nominative has @deva[-ः], accusative has @deva[-म्]

---

## concept - Neuter a-stems (फल type)

Neuters use @deva[-म्] for BOTH nominative and accusative:

| Case | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|------|----------|------|--------|
| Nom. | @deva[फलम्] | @deva[फले] | @deva[फलानि] |
| Acc. | @deva[फलम्] | @deva[फले] | @deva[फलानि] |

Neuter nominative = accusative (always!)

---

## concept - Feminine ā-stems (कन्या type)

| Case | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|------|----------|------|--------|
| Nom. | @deva[कन्या] | @deva[कन्ये] | @deva[कन्याः] |
| Acc. | @deva[कन्याम्] | @deva[कन्ये] | @deva[कन्याः] |

**Pattern:** Singular accusative adds @deva[-म्] to the stem

---

## concept - Recognition Guide

When reading, spot cases by endings:

**Nominative clues:**
- @deva[-ः] — masculine singular
- @deva[-म्] — neuter singular
- @deva[-आ] — feminine singular
- @deva[-आः] — masculine/feminine plural

**Accusative clues:**
- @deva[-म्] — singular (all genders)
- @deva[-न्] — masculine plural
- @deva[-आनि] — neuter plural

---

## concept - Practice Parsing

Parse these sentences:

1. @deva[देवाः फलानि खादन्ति]
   - @deva[देवाः] = nom. pl. (the gods — subject)
   - @deva[फलानि] = acc. pl. (the fruits — object)
   - "The gods eat fruits"

2. @deva[कन्या पुस्तकं पठति]
   - @deva[कन्या] = nom. sg. (the girl)
   - @deva[पुस्तकम्] = acc. sg. (the book)
   - "The girl reads the book"

---

## concept - Lesson Summary

Two cases handle most sentences:
- **Nominative** = subject (who does it)
- **Accusative** = object (what's done to)

Key markers:
- Nom. sg. masc.: @deva[-ः]
- Acc. sg.: @deva[-म्]
- Neuter: nom. = acc.
