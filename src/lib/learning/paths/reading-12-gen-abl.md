## concept - Genitive Case (षष्ठी)

The genitive shows **possession** or **relation** — "of":

@deva[रामस्य पुत्रः] — "Rama's son" / "the son **of** Rama"

---

## concept - Genitive Endings

| Gender | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|--------|----------|------|--------|
| Masc. (देव) | @deva[देवस्य] | @deva[देवयोः] | @deva[देवानाम्] |
| Neut. (फल) | @deva[फलस्य] | @deva[फलयोः] | @deva[फलानाम्] |
| Fem. (कन्या) | @deva[कन्यायाः] | @deva[कन्ययोः] | @deva[कन्यानाम्] |

**Key marker:** @deva[-स्य] (sg. masc/neut), @deva[-आनाम्] (pl.)

---

## concept - Genitive Uses

**1. Possession:**
- @deva[नृपस्य गृहम्] — "the king's house"
- @deva[वनस्य मध्ये] — "in the middle of the forest"

**2. Partitive:**
- @deva[तेषां एकः] — "one of them"
- @deva[सर्वेषां श्रेष्ठः] — "best of all"

---

## concept - Ablative Case (पञ्चमी)

The ablative shows **source**, **separation**, or **cause** — "from":

@deva[ग्रामात् आगच्छति] — "comes **from** the village"

---

## concept - Ablative Endings

| Gender | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|--------|----------|------|--------|
| Masc. (देव) | @deva[देवात्] | @deva[देवाभ्याम्] | @deva[देवेभ्यः] |
| Neut. (फल) | @deva[फलात्] | @deva[फलाभ्याम्] | @deva[फलेभ्यः] |
| Fem. (कन्या) | @deva[कन्यायाः] | @deva[कन्याभ्याम्] | @deva[कन्याभ्यः] |

**Key marker:** @deva[-आत्] (sg. masc/neut)

---

## concept - Ablative Uses

**1. Source/Origin:**
- @deva[नगरात् आगच्छति] — "comes from the city"
- @deva[वृक्षात् पतति] — "falls from the tree"

**2. Cause:**
- @deva[भयात् पलायते] — "flees from fear"
- @deva[दुःखात् रोदिति] — "cries from sorrow"

**3. Comparison:**
- @deva[रामात् बलवान्] — "stronger than Rama"

---

## concept - Genitive vs Ablative

| Genitive (षष्ठी) | Ablative (पञ्चमी) |
|------------------|-------------------|
| "of" — relation | "from" — source |
| @deva[राजस्य] "of the king" | @deva[राजात्] "from the king" |
| @deva[-स्य] ending | @deva[-आत्] ending |

---

## concept - Practice

1. @deva[तस्य पुत्रः वनं गच्छति]
   - "**His** son goes to the forest"

2. @deva[ग्रामात् नगरं गच्छति]
   - "Goes **from** village to city"

3. @deva[विद्यायाः फलं सुखम्]
   - "The fruit **of** learning is happiness"

---

## concept - Lesson Summary

Two related cases:
- **Genitive** (@deva[षष्ठी]): "of" — @deva[-स्य]
- **Ablative** (@deva[पञ्चमी]): "from" — @deva[-आत्]
