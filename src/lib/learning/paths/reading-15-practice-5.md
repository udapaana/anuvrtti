## reading - Time Devours All

**Source:** Traditional Subhāṣita

@deva[कालः पचति भूतानि कालः संहरते प्रजाः]

Split:
- @deva[कालः पचति भूतानि] — no sandhi
- @deva[कालः संहरते प्रजाः] — no sandhi

Parse:
- @deva[कालः] — "Time" (nom.)
- @deva[पचति] — "cooks/ripens" (present)
- @deva[भूतानि] — "beings" (acc. pl. neut.)
- @deva[संहरते] — "gathers up" (middle voice)
- @deva[प्रजाः] — "creatures" (acc. pl.)

**Translation:** "Time cooks beings; Time gathers up creatures."

---

## reading - Time Never Sleeps

@deva[कालः सुप्तेषु जागर्ति कालो हि दुरतिक्रमः]

Split:
- @deva[कालो हि] = @deva[कालः हि] (visarga sandhi — we'll learn this)
- @deva[दुरतिक्रमः] = @deva[दुः + अतिक्रमः] (compound)

Parse:
- @deva[सुप्तेषु] — "while [beings are] asleep" (loc. pl.)
- @deva[जागर्ति] — "stays awake"
- @deva[हि] — "indeed"
- @deva[दुरतिक्रमः] — "hard to cross over"

**Translation:** "Time stays awake while [all] sleep; Time indeed is hard to overcome."

---

## concept - Locative Absolute

@deva[सुप्तेषु] is a **locative absolute** — "while X-ing":

@deva[सुप्तेषु जागर्ति] = "while [they are] sleeping, [Time] stays awake"

The locative can express simultaneous action.

## reading - Truth and Kindness

**Source:** Manusmṛti

@deva[सत्यं ब्रूयात् प्रियं ब्रूयात् न ब्रूयात् सत्यमप्रियम्]

Split:
- @deva[सत्यमप्रियम्] = @deva[सत्यम् अप्रियम्] (a + a → ā)

Parse:
- @deva[सत्यम्] — "truth" (acc.)
- @deva[ब्रूयात्] — "one should speak" (optative)
- @deva[प्रियम्] — "pleasant" (acc.)
- @deva[अप्रियम्] — "unpleasant"

**Translation:** "One should speak truth; one should speak pleasantly; one should not speak truth that is unpleasant."

---

## reading - Continued

@deva[प्रियं च नानृतं ब्रूयात् एष धर्मः सनातनः]

Split:
- @deva[नानृतम्] = @deva[न अनृतम्] (a + a → ā)

Parse:
- @deva[प्रियम्] — "pleasant"
- @deva[च] — "and"
- @deva[अनृतम्] — "falsehood"
- @deva[एषः] — "this"
- @deva[धर्मः] — "dharma"
- @deva[सनातनः] — "eternal"

**Translation:** "Nor should one speak pleasant falsehood — this is the eternal dharma."

---

## concept - The Optative Mood

@deva[ब्रूयात्] "one should speak" is the **optative** — expressing what ought to be done:

- @deva[गच्छेत्] — "one should go"
- @deva[वदेत्] — "one should speak"
- @deva[कुर्यात्] — "one should do"

We'll learn this fully later.
