## reading - The Foolish Princes

**Source:** Pañcatantra 1.intro.2

@deva[तस्य त्रयः पुत्राः परमदुर्मेधसः बभूवुः]

Parse:
- @deva[तस्य] — "his" (genitive — we'll learn this soon)
- @deva[त्रयः] — "three" (nominative plural)
- @deva[पुत्राः] — "sons" (nominative plural)
- @deva[परमदुर्मेधसः] — "extremely dull-witted" (nominative plural)
- @deva[बभूवुः] — "were" (past, plural)

**Translation:** "His three sons were extremely dull-witted."

## reading - The King Sees

@deva[तान् दृष्ट्वा राजा चिन्तयामास]

Parse:
- @deva[तान्] — "them" (accusative plural)
- @deva[दृष्ट्वा] — "having seen" (absolutive — we'll learn this)
- @deva[राजा] — "the king" (nominative)
- @deva[चिन्तयामास] — "reflected/thought"

**Translation:** "Having seen them, the king reflected."

## reading - The Merchant Sets Out

**Source:** Pañcatantra 1.1.1

@deva[अस्ति मथुरायां वर्धमानो नाम वणिक्]

- @deva[अस्ति] — "there is"
- @deva[मथुरायाम्] — "in Mathura" (locative)
- @deva[वर्धमानः] — "Vardhamāna" (nominative, name)
- @deva[नाम] — "named"
- @deva[वणिक्] — "a merchant" (nominative)

**Translation:** "In Mathura there is a merchant named Vardhamāna."

## reading - Taking Wealth

@deva[स एकदा बहुधनं गृहीत्वा व्यापाराय प्रस्थितः]

- @deva[सः] — "he" (nominative)
- @deva[एकदा] — "one day"
- @deva[बहुधनम्] — "much wealth" (accusative)
- @deva[गृहीत्वा] — "having taken"
- @deva[व्यापाराय] — "for trade" (dative — purpose)
- @deva[प्रस्थितः] — "set out"

**Translation:** "One day, having taken much wealth, he set out for trade."

---

## concept - Patterns You're Seeing

1. **Nominative plural** in @deva[-आः]: @deva[पुत्राः], @deva[देवाः]
2. **Accusative plural** in @deva[-आन्]: @deva[तान्] (them)
3. **Story formula**: @deva[अस्ति] + place + @deva[नाम] + character
4. **Sequence words**: @deva[एकदा] (one day), @deva[तत्र] (there)

---

## concept - Lesson Summary

You're reading real Pañcatantra prose! You can now:
- Spot nominative subjects (@deva[-ः], @deva[-आः])
- Spot accusative objects (@deva[-म्], @deva[-आन्])
- Recognize story opening formulas
- Follow basic narrative flow
