## concept - The Dative Case

The dative (@deva[चतुर्थी]) expresses:
- **Recipient**: "to" someone
- **Purpose**: "for" something
- **Benefit**: "for the sake of"

@deva[बालकाय ददाति] — "gives **to** the boy"

---

## concept - Dative Endings

| Gender | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|--------|----------|------|--------|
| Masc. (देव) | @deva[देवाय] | @deva[देवाभ्याम्] | @deva[देवेभ्यः] |
| Neut. (फल) | @deva[फलाय] | @deva[फलाभ्याम्] | @deva[फलेभ्यः] |
| Fem. (कन्या) | @deva[कन्यायै] | @deva[कन्याभ्याम्] | @deva[कन्याभ्यः] |

**Key marker:** @deva[-आय] (sg. masc/neut), @deva[-एभ्यः] (pl.)

---

## concept - Meanings of Dative

**1. Indirect object (recipient):**
- @deva[गुरवे नमति] — "bows to the teacher"
- @deva[देवाय फलं ददाति] — "gives fruit to the god"

**2. Purpose:**
- @deva[व्यापाराय गच्छति] — "goes for trade"
- @deva[जलाय प्रयाति] — "goes for water"

**3. Benefit:**
- @deva[पुत्राय धनं रक्षति] — "protects wealth for (his) son"

---

## concept - Practice Sentences

1. @deva[नरः देवाय पुष्पं यच्छति]
   - "The man offers a flower **to the god**"

2. @deva[राजा प्रजाभ्यः वदति]
   - "The king speaks **to the people**"

3. @deva[अध्ययनाय गच्छामि]
   - "I go **for study**"
