## concept - The Locative Case

The locative (@deva[सप्तमी]) shows **where** or **when**:

@deva[गृहे वसति] — "lives **in** the house"
@deva[प्रातः काले] — "**at** morning time"

---

## concept - Locative Endings

| Gender | Singular | Dual | Plural |
|--------|----------|------|--------|
| Masc. (देव) | @deva[देवे] | @deva[देवयोः] | @deva[देवेषु] |
| Neut. (फल) | @deva[फले] | @deva[फलयोः] | @deva[फलेषु] |
| Fem. (कन्या) | @deva[कन्यायाम्] | @deva[कन्ययोः] | @deva[कन्यासु] |

**Key markers:** @deva[-ए] (sg. masc/neut), @deva[-एषु/-आसु] (pl.)

---

## concept - Locative Uses

**1. Place:**
- @deva[वने गच्छति] — "goes in the forest"
- @deva[नगरे वसति] — "lives in the city"

**2. Time:**
- @deva[रात्रौ] — "at night"
- @deva[वर्षे] — "in a year"

**3. Circumstance:**
- @deva[युद्धे] — "in battle"
- @deva[आपदि] — "in calamity"

## concept - All Seven Cases Summary

| Case | Name | Meaning | Marker |
|------|------|---------|--------|
| 1st | @deva[प्रथमा] | subject | @deva[-ः] |
| 2nd | @deva[द्वितीया] | object | @deva[-म्] |
| 3rd | @deva[तृतीया] | by/with | @deva[-एन] |
| 4th | @deva[चतुर्थी] | to/for | @deva[-आय] |
| 5th | @deva[पञ्चमी] | from | @deva[-आत्] |
| 6th | @deva[षष्ठी] | of | @deva[-स्य] |
| 7th | @deva[सप्तमी] | in/on/at | @deva[-ए] |

---

## concept - Practice

1. @deva[बालकः वृक्षे तिष्ठति]
   - "The boy stands **on** the tree"

2. @deva[ग्रामेषु जनाः वसन्ति]
   - "People live **in** the villages"

3. @deva[तस्मिन् काले राजा आसीत्]
   - "**At** that time there was a king"
