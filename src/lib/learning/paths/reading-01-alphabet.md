## concept - The Sanskrit Alphabet

Sanskrit has 46 basic sounds organized systematically:

**Vowels (@deva[स्वराः])** — 13 sounds
**Consonants (@deva[व्यञ्जनानि])** — 33 sounds

The alphabet is called @deva[वर्णमाला] — "garland of letters."

---

## concept - Vowels (स्वराः)

Simple vowels come in short and long pairs:

| Short | Long | Sound |
|-------|------|-------|
| @deva[अ] | @deva[आ] | a / ā |
| @deva[इ] | @deva[ई] | i / ī |
| @deva[उ] | @deva[ऊ] | u / ū |
| @deva[ऋ] | @deva[ॠ] | ṛ / ṝ |
| @deva[ऌ] | — | ḷ |

Compound vowels (diphthongs):
| Letter | Sound |
|--------|-------|
| @deva[ए] | e |
| @deva[ऐ] | ai |
| @deva[ओ] | o |
| @deva[औ] | au |

## concept - Consonants: Stops (स्पर्शाः)

Organized by mouth position and voicing:

|  | Unvoiced | Unvoiced Aspirated | Voiced | Voiced Aspirated | Nasal |
|--|----------|-------------------|--------|-----------------|-------|
| Velar | @deva[क] ka | @deva[ख] kha | @deva[ग] ga | @deva[घ] gha | @deva[ङ] ṅa |
| Palatal | @deva[च] ca | @deva[छ] cha | @deva[ज] ja | @deva[झ] jha | @deva[ञ] ña |
| Retroflex | @deva[ट] ṭa | @deva[ठ] ṭha | @deva[ड] ḍa | @deva[ढ] ḍha | @deva[ण] ṇa |
| Dental | @deva[त] ta | @deva[थ] tha | @deva[द] da | @deva[ध] dha | @deva[न] na |
| Labial | @deva[प] pa | @deva[फ] pha | @deva[ब] ba | @deva[भ] bha | @deva[म] ma |

---

## concept - Other Consonants

**Semivowels (@deva[अन्तःस्थाः])**
@deva[य] ya, @deva[र] ra, @deva[ल] la, @deva[व] va

**Sibilants (@deva[ऊष्माणः])**
@deva[श] śa (palatal), @deva[ष] ṣa (retroflex), @deva[स] sa (dental)

**Aspirate**
@deva[ह] ha

**Special signs**
@deva[ं] anusvāra (nasal), @deva[ः] visarga (breath)
