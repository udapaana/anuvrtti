## concept - The Visarga (ः)

The visarga @deva[ः] is a breath sound at word end. It changes based on what follows.

@deva[नरः गच्छति] — visarga stays
@deva[नरो वदति] — visarga → @deva[ओ]

---

## concept - Visarga Before Vowels

Before @deva[अ], visarga often disappears and @deva[अ] lengthens:

| Before | Change | Example |
|--------|--------|---------|
| @deva[अ] | @deva[ः अ] → @deva[ो ऽ] | @deva[नरः अस्ति] → @deva[नरोऽस्ति] |

The @deva[ऽ] (avagraha) shows a dropped @deva[अ].

---

## concept - Visarga Before Voiced Sounds

Before voiced consonants and vowels (except @deva[अ]):

| Before | Change | Example |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Voiced cons. | @deva[ः] → @deva[ओ/र्] | @deva[देवः ददाति] → @deva[देवो ददाति] |
| @deva[आ, इ, उ...] | @deva[ः] → @deva[र्] | @deva[पुनः आगच्छति] → @deva[पुनरागच्छति] |

---

## concept - Visarga Before Unvoiced Sounds

Before unvoiced consonants, visarga often becomes @deva[श्/ष्/स्]:

| Before | Change | Example |
|--------|--------|---------|
| @deva[च/छ] | @deva[ः] → @deva[श्] | @deva[नरः च] → @deva[नरश्च] |
| @deva[ट/ठ] | @deva[ः] → @deva[ष्] | @deva[नरः टीकते] → @deva[नरष्टीकते] |
| @deva[त/थ] | @deva[ः] → @deva[स्] | @deva[नरः तत्र] → @deva[नरस्तत्र] |
| @deva[क/ख/प/फ] | @deva[ः] stays | @deva[नरः करोति] (no change) |

---

## concept - Common Patterns

Memorize these frequent patterns:

| Written | Split |
|---------|-------|
| @deva[नमस्ते] | @deva[नमः ते] |
| @deva[नरश्च] | @deva[नरः च] |
| @deva[कालो हि] | @deva[कालः हि] |
| @deva[तपोवनम्] | @deva[तपः वनम्] |
| @deva[मनोहरम्] | @deva[मनः हरम्] |

---

## concept - Reading Strategy

When you see @deva[ो], @deva[र्], @deva[श्], @deva[ष्], or @deva[स्] connecting words, consider if it was originally @deva[ः]:

@deva[देवो वदति] ← @deva[देवः वदति]
@deva[पुनरागच्छति] ← @deva[पुनः आगच्छति]
@deva[नमस्कारः] ← @deva[नमः कारः]

---

## concept - Practice Splitting

Split these:

1. @deva[रामश्च लक्ष्मणश्च]
   - @deva[रामः च लक्ष्मणः च] — "Rama and Lakṣmaṇa"

2. @deva[तपोवने]
   - @deva[तपः वने] — "in the ascetic's forest"

3. @deva[मनोरथः]
   - @deva[मनः रथः] — "wish" (lit. "mind's chariot")

4. @deva[पुनरुक्तम्]
   - @deva[पुनः उक्तम्] — "said again"

---

## concept - Lesson Summary

Visarga sandhi rules:
1. Before @deva[अ]: @deva[ः अ] → @deva[ो ऽ]
2. Before voiced: @deva[ः] → @deva[ओ/र्]
3. Before @deva[च/त]: @deva[ः] → @deva[श्/स्]
4. Before @deva[क/प]: @deva[ः] stays
