## 3.3.173 - Āśiṣi liṅloṭau

**Key Terms:** ASizi, liN

The suffixes liṅ and loṭ express āśīḥ (blessing/benediction). Benedictive mood.

---

## 3.4.116 - Liṅāśiṣi

**Key Terms:** liN, ASizi, yAsut

In āśīḥ usage, the augment 'yāsuṭ' is added after the dhātu. Creates distinctive blessing forms.

---

## 7.2.79 - Liṅāśiṣi iṭ

**Key Terms:** liN, ASizi, iT

In liṅ āśīḥ, the augment 'iṭ' is inserted. Differentiates from regular optative.

---

## 3.4.104 - Kintve sīyuṭ

**Key Terms:** sIyuT, Atmanepade

In ātmanepada benedictive, 'sīyuṭ' (= sī) is the marker. Middle voice blessing.

---

## 7.2.80 - Āto guṇe

**Key Terms:** AtaH, guRe

When guṇa applies after 'ā', specific changes occur. Affects benedictive vowels.

---

## 3.4.117 - Yāsuṭ parasmaipadeṣu

**Key Terms:** yAsut, parasmEpadezu

In parasmaipada, 'yāsuṭ' (= yās) creates forms like 'bhūyāt'. The blessing formula.

---

## 7.2.81 - Āto dīrgho yañi

**Key Terms:** AtaH, dIrGa, yaN

Before 'y' of yaṅ-class affixes, final 'ā' of stem lengthens. Vowel adjustment.

---

## 6.4.67 - Sasya lopaḥ śiti

**Key Terms:** sasya, lopaH, Siti

The 's' of 'yās' may be deleted before 'ś'-initial endings. Creates contracted forms.
