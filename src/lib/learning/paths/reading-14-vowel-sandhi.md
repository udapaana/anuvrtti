## concept - What is Sandhi?

When Sanskrit words come together, their sounds often merge. This is **sandhi** (@deva[सन्धि] "joining").

@deva[च + अपि] → @deva[चापि] (a + a → ā)
@deva[न + इति] → @deva[नेति] (a + i → e)

---

## concept - Similar Vowels Merge

When two similar vowels meet, they become one long vowel:

| Meeting | Result | Example |
|---------|--------|---------|
| a + a | ā | @deva[न अस्ति] → @deva[नास्ति] |
| i + i | ī | @deva[गच्छति इति] → @deva[गच्छतीति] |
| u + u | ū | @deva[साधु उक्तम्] → @deva[साधूक्तम्] |

---

## concept - Dissimilar Vowels: Guṇa

When @deva[अ/आ] meets @deva[इ/ई], @deva[उ/ऊ], or @deva[ऋ], they form guṇa:

| Meeting | Result | Example |
|---------|--------|---------|
| a + i/ī | e | @deva[च इति] → @deva[चेति] |
| a + u/ū | o | @deva[न उचितम्] → @deva[नोचितम्] |
| a + ṛ | ar | @deva[महा ऋषिः] → @deva[महर्षिः] |

---

## concept - Dissimilar Vowels: Vṛddhi

When @deva[आ] meets @deva[ए/ऐ] or @deva[ओ/औ]:

| Meeting | Result | Example |
|---------|--------|---------|
| ā + e | ai | @deva[सदा एव] → @deva[सदैव] |
| ā + o | au | @deva[महा ओजस्] → @deva[महौजस्] |

---

## concept - i/u Become Semivowels

When @deva[इ/ई] or @deva[उ/ऊ] come before a different vowel:

| Meeting | Result | Example |
|---------|--------|---------|
| i + a | ya | @deva[इति अपि] → @deva[इत्यपि] |
| u + a | va | @deva[मधु अस्ति] → @deva[मध्वस्ति] |

@deva[इ] → @deva[य्], @deva[उ] → @deva[व्]

---

## concept - Reading Through Sandhi

When reading, mentally split compounds:

| Written | Split | Meaning |
|---------|-------|---------|
| @deva[नास्ति] | @deva[न अस्ति] | "is not" |
| @deva[चेति] | @deva[च इति] | "and thus" |
| @deva[तत्रैव] | @deva[तत्र एव] | "right there" |
| @deva[इत्युक्त्वा] | @deva[इति उक्त्वा] | "having said thus" |

---

## concept - Common Sandhi Patterns

Memorize these frequent combinations:

| Pattern | Example |
|---------|---------|
| @deva[च + अपि] → @deva[चापि] | "and also" |
| @deva[न + अस्ति] → @deva[नास्ति] | "is not" |
| @deva[इति + उक्तम्] → @deva[इत्युक्तम्] | "thus said" |
| @deva[तत्र + एव] → @deva[तत्रैव] | "right there" |

---

## concept - Lesson Summary

Vowel sandhi rules:
1. **Same vowel** → long: a+a=ā, i+i=ī
2. **a + i/u** → guṇa: e, o
3. **ā + e/o** → vṛddhi: ai, au
4. **i/u + vowel** → y/v + vowel
