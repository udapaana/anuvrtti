# Vasu Commentary Rewrite

Rewriting Vasu's Aṣṭādhyāyī commentary with William Zinsser-esque clarity and simplicity.

## Principles

1. **Clarity over verbosity** — Say it once, say it right
2. **Structure over prose** — Use tables, lists, examples where words would clutter
3. **No information loss** — Every technical detail preserved
4. **Consistent markup** — `@deva[...]`, `@ref[...]`, `@note[...]`, `@[...]` for iast
